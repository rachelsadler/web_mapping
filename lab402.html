One of my favorite recipes: Teriyaki Salmon

This is a recipe I have found and adapted from Simply Home Cooked.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Brown sugar 1/2 cup
Garlic 1 Tbsp minced
Red pepper flakes 1 tsp
Soy sauce 1/2 cup
Teriyaki sauce 1/2 cup
Olive oil 2 tsp
Green onions 1/2 cup chopped
Corn starch 1 Tbsp
Salmon 4 fillets

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine brown sugar, garlic, soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, and red pepper flakes. Set aside 1/2 cup in a small sauce pan.
  2. In the bowl add the olive oil and chopped green onion.
  3. Place the salmon fillets in a container with a lid and pour the bowl with the marinade into the container. Put the lid on and store in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  4. Mix the 1 Tbsp of corn starch with 1 Tbsp of water in a small bowl. Once this has combined, add it to the 1/2 cup of marinade in the small sauce pan. Cook on medium-low heat, stirring contantly, until it begins to thicken into a glaze.
  5. After the salmon has been marinating for 30 minutes, cook the salmon in an air fryer at 400 degree fahrenheit for 8 minutes.
  6. When the salmon is done, serve with glaze and enjoy!
